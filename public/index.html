What Tree is This?

Information

Images are drawn from the USDA website and Wikimedia Commons. This site is for identifying Brooklyn and Manhattan street trees and will not work in city parks or outside these two boroughs. The appearance of trees within a certain species varies drastically based on time of year and environmental conditions such as soil quality and light exposure, and thus the above picture may not perfectly reflect the tree in front of you.


Keep in mind that the data from which these conclusions are made is updated by the NYC Parks Department "as needed," and experience has shown that many street trees are not available in the Parks Department database. Further reseach is required to be certain of this identification. Everyone should have a quality tree-identification book, such as Trees of North America.
